⛏️ Minecraft Guide

Commands, enchantments, redstone tutorials, survival tips & creative building.

Essential Commands

Enable cheats in world settings. Press T to open chat:

CommandDescription
/gamemode creativeSwitch to Creative mode
/gamemode survivalSwitch to Survival mode
/time set daySet time to day
/weather clearClear weather
/give @p diamond 64Give 64 diamonds
/tp @p ~ ~10 ~Teleport 10 blocks up
/effect give @p speed 999 2Speed II for 999s
/locate structure strongholdFind nearest stronghold
/kill @e[type=!player]Remove all entities
/setworldspawnSet world spawn here

First Night Survival

  1. Punch trees — 16+ oak logs
  2. Craft tools — table, pickaxe, axe, sword
  3. Find food — kill animals or find villages
  4. Mine stone — upgrade before sunset
  5. Build shelter — 5×5 with a door
  6. Light up — torches prevent mob spawns
  7. Find iron — Y-level 16

Best Enchantments

  • Sword: Sharpness V, Looting III, Unbreaking III, Mending
  • Pickaxe: Efficiency V, Fortune III, Unbreaking III, Mending
  • Armor: Protection IV on all pieces
  • Bow: Power V, Infinity, Flame

🦙 Palworld Guide

Pal catching strategies, base building, breeding, combat tips & server multiplayer.

Pal Catching Tips

  • Weaken first: Get Pal to low HP (red) before throwing a Pal Sphere
  • At night: Pals are drowsy and easier to catch
  • Higher tier spheres: Mega / Giga / Hyper spheres dramatically improve catch rate
  • Status effects: Sleeping or burned Pals are much easier to catch
  • Syndicate Tower: Defeat boss Pals for rare eggs, not direct catches

Best Starter Pals

PalTypeWhy
LamballNeutralBest early wool/work pal
CattivaNeutralIncreases carrying capacity
FoxparksFireDoubles as portable flamethrower
PengulletIce/WaterGreat early combat + watering
LifmunkGrassGreat for planting and shooting

Base Building Tips

  1. Build your Palbox on flat ground with room to expand
  2. Assign Pals to work based on their suitability stars
  3. Always have Pals assigned to kindling, watering & gathering
  4. Build a Feed Box early — hungry Pals work slowly
  5. Hot Springs are essential — rest restores Pal sanity
  6. Multi-story bases save space on limited flat terrain

Combat & Weapons

  • Early: Musket + fire arrow combo for boss fights
  • Mid: Assault Rifle with Katakamas for aggressive play
  • Pal attacks: Always active-skill your combat Pal during boss fights
  • Passive skills: Breed Pals with "Legend", "Ferocious", "Brave" traits

🏏 Cricket 26 Guide

Batting techniques, bowling strategies, field settings & Career Mode tips.

Batting Controls

ShotButtonWhen to Use
DriveA / XFull-length balls outside off-stump
Cut ShotB / CircleShort balls outside off-stump
Pull ShotY / TriangleShort balls on leg side
SweepLB + ASpinners bowling full on leg
Lofted DriveHold AClearing the boundary in powerplay
DefensiveX / SquareGood-length balls on off-stump

Bowling Tips

  • Pace variation: Use slower balls (hold LT/L2) against aggressive batsmen
  • Swing: Bowl full-length to swing the ball early in a match
  • Spinners: Bowl in the footmarks on day 4-5 in Test matches
  • Yorkers: Aim at the batsman's feet at the death overs
  • Bouncers: Use 1-2 per over to keep batsmen uncomfortable

Career Mode Tips

  1. Choose a strong domestic team to get more opportunities early
  2. Focus on one batting style (aggressive vs defensive) and master it
  3. Always practice in the nets before big matches to raise confidence
  4. In T20s, save your best batsmen for the last 5 overs
  5. Select weather conditions favouring your playing style
  6. Build fitness by playing all match types — don't skip county games
